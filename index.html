A superior iOS listening experience.

SpotCompiled offers versions of EeveeSpotify and Sposify IPAs. EeveeSpotify is an iOS app tweak for Spotify that eliminates ads, removes the limitation on skips, and unlocks nearly all premium features, with the exception of offline downloads, which are managed server-side. Sposify is another iOS tweak that introduces a variety of enhancements to the Spotify app, further enriching the user experience.
